### Key Discovery: Core-Coupled InstructionSets

Three InstructionSets (`V30MZ`, `ARM7TDMI`, `SPC700`) are deeply referenced by `SemanticAnalyzer`, `CodeGenerator`, `GbaRomBuilder`, and `SpcFileBuilder` in `Poppy.Core`. Moving them would create circular dependencies (Arch ‚Üí Core ‚Üí Arch). These stay in Core with `InternalsVisibleTo` for their respective Arch projects.
